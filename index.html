John Yoshio Naka

A great bonsai teacher in the west

John Yoshio Naka with his Juniper forest style bonsai 'Goshin'

    Here's a time line of John Naka's life:

  • 1914 - Born Ft. Lupton, Colorado
  • 1922 - Moved to his parents home country of Japan
  • 1935 - Returned to United States near Boulder, Colorado
  • 1946 - Settled in Los Angeles, California
  • 1950 - Started California Bonsai Society with four friends
  • 1950s and 1960s - Became a very important force in American bonsai art
  • 1967 - Received an honorary medal and citation from the Japanese Agricultural Affairs Department
  • 1973 - Published Bonsai Techiques I
  • 1982 - Published Bonsai Techiques II
  • 1985 - Awarded The Fifth Class of the Order of the Rising Sun by Emperor Hirohito of Japan.
  • 2004 - Death
  • 2009 - Awarded Pacific Pioneer Award
